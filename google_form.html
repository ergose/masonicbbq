Smoked Boston Pork Butt (shoulder) Sale 2015

Salem Lodge No. 289 of Winston-Salem and Kernersville Lodge No. 669 of Kernersville and Bivouac Lodge #503 will hold smoked butt sale for pick up on Saturday, April 25 at three locations.

The Boston Butt cut is from a pork shoulder and cooked weights will vary between 4-7 pounds. The cost of the whole butt will be $35 and this includes a pint of sauce. The butts will be slow smoked with hickory wood. They will be ready for pickup between 9:00 a.m. and noon on Saturday, April 25, 2015. The pickup locations are:

New Philadelphia Moravian Church parking lot at 4440 Country Club Rd, Winston-Salem, 27104, or

Kernersville Lodge No.669, 701 S. Main Street, Kernersville, 27284, or

Bivouac Lodge #503, 1025 14th St NE Winston-Salem, NC 27105

For Salem and Kernersville Lodges, all proceeds from the sale will be evenly divided between two 501(c)(3) Masonic charities: Masonic Home for Children at Oxford, Inc., www.mhc-oxford.org and NC Masonic and Eastern Star Home Foundation, LLC, www.liveatwhitestone.org.

For Bivouac Lodge, proceeds from the sale will benefit Central Children's Home in Oxford, NC, a 501(c)(3) charity.

On your order form below, please mark at which location you wish to pick up your order. Reheating directions will be included with your order. Also mark your order which lodge is to receive credit for your order.

If you have questions, in Winston-Salem please contact: George Troxler, 336-768-0588, gtroxler@gmail.com (West Winston Area). Rodney Funderburk, 336-790-2480, Rodney@b-lak.com (East Winston Area)
In Kernersville please contact: Chad Delp, 336-413-8440, chad@chaddelp.com

Paper orders may be mailed to: George Troxler, 404 Pennsylvania Avenue, Winston-Salem, NC 27104

All orders must be turned in by Wednesday, April 15.
